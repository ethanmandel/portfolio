A little behind the lens.

I got into design through photography, music, and spending way too much time noticing how people interact with things. I like work that feels thoughtful, visually strong, and actually enjoyable to use.

Studying Informatics and Human-Computer Interaction at University of California, Irvine gave me the technical side of design, but a lot of my perspective comes from creative work outside of school. Photography taught me composition and attention to detail. Music taught me patience, experimentation, and rhythm.

Outside of design, you’ll probably find me behind a camera, on a bike ride, or lost in my favorite album.
